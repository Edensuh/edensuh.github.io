Audio doc: Eden Suh explores Northern Ontario's exceedingly high rate of tinnitus

This story was a part of my Master's Research Project that I did for my degree. I explored the higher rates of tinnitus in Northern Ontario and how it may mentally affect people with the condition.

I did the scripting, recording, interviews and editing (everything).

I did the scripting, VO, shooting, and editing for this video (everything).

Video: Pitter-patter, let's get at 'er: Letterkenny gifts outdoor gym to the Nickel City

This was a story on the grand opening of Pitter Patter Park here in Sudbury. I was initially sent to cover the event of the opening ceremony but after everyone had left, I saw an opportunity to film people actually using the outdoor gym. I quickly asked the young adults if they would be interested in being in a video and they gave me the greenlight.

I did the scripting, VO, shooting, and editing for this video (everything).
