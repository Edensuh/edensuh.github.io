About Me

Hello! I’m Eden Suh, a versatile digital communications professional with a passion for storytelling, audience engagement, and creating impactful content across diverse platforms. With a background in journalism and digital media, I thrive at the intersection of creativity and strategy, crafting stories that connect with audiences in meaningful ways.

My career began with a foundation in journalism, where I worked as a New Media Reporter for Sudbury.com. There, I honed my skills in researching, writing, and producing stories for digital and social platforms. Whether it was breaking news, video content, or one-minute TikTok updates, I quickly adapted to the fast-paced demands of digital-first storytelling while developing a strong on-camera presence.

Currently, as a Digital Communications Advisor for Health Canada, I collaborate with stakeholders to create content that aligns with government standards and engages diverse audiences. My role involves everything from editing web content to ensuring campaigns resonate across multiple channels. This experience has further refined my attention to detail, time management, and ability to communicate complex topics in accessible ways.

Along the way, I’ve also worked as a Digital Content Producer for Evermore Capital Inc., creating innovative marketing content that blends visuals and messaging to tell compelling stories. My ability to adapt to new tools, ensures that my work remains cutting-edge and effective in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

What sets me apart is my ability to see the big picture while diving into the details that matter. I excel in transforming trending topics and data insights into consumable content that captures attention and drives engagement. Whether collaborating with a team, launching new initiatives, or managing campaigns, I bring creativity, curiosity, and a can-do attitude to every project.

If you’re looking for someone who’s passionate about storytelling, adept at connecting with audiences, and ready to push boundaries in content creation, I’d love to hear from you. Let’s create something exceptional together!
